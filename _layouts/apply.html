Pledge Education Program Application.

Requirements

Applicants who do not meet the following requirements will not be considered.


1. Minimum GPA of 2.5

2. Current class standing of second semester freshman or higher

3. Current full-time student at NJIT

Application Material

Prepare the following documents for your application.


1. Resume

Please include:

  • Professional experience

  • Academic experience

  • Volunteer/extracurricular experience


2. Professional Headshot

Image should display shoulders and above


3. Unofficial Transcript

Preferably in PDF format

4. Cover Letter

In your cover letter, strive to answer the following questions:

  • Introductions/who are you?

  • Why are you interested in joining Alpha Kappa Psi?

  • What qualifies you to be a member of Alpha Kappa Psi?

  • What do you think you can contribute to Alpha Kappa Psi?
